The Grizzly Bear Diet
Check out more about grizzly bears

Grizzly bears are omnivores which means that they eat plants, fruits, and meats. The common plants they eat are roots, berries, and grass. As for meats, they eat fish, rodents, and animals like deer, elk, and moose.
